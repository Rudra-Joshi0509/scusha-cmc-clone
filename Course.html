Course Details

The duration of the course will be of three full time academic years. The examination for the Bachelor of Home Science course will be divided into six semesters. Candidate who have passed an equivalent examination from any other board or examining body and is seeking admission to the Home Science course will be required to provide necessary eligibility certificate.

Eligibility

Any Candidate having completed 10 + 2 from any recognized Education Board.

Duration

3 Year - 6 Semester Full Time Course

Course Details

It is a three-year undergraduate course that can be pursued by the student who is aspiring their minds in Arts field. The duration of the course is 3 years and divided into 6 semesters. It comprises of the subjects like English , History, Gujarati , Economics and Hindi. Course Details

The duration of the course is full time two academic years. The examination for the Master of Home Science course will be conducted under the semester system.
