About The Management

The Surendranagar Law Society started in 1986 for the purpose of education, which is helpful in self-employment. First Law society started Law College, in co-education, so that one can became self dependent, after one year Law Society started Mahila Home Science College, because being backward area, parents don't allow their daughters to study in Co-education College.

The mission is to develop self-confidence, became independent and make them science oriented. A girl with scientific approach and mother of future generation can make their future generation intellectual. To inculcate values & overall development of girls is the mission of our institution.

" Ours is only Mahila College imparting, Home Science, Arts, Commerce, and M.Sc. Home Science in Surendranagar district. "
